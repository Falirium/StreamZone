# StreamZone Passes: Marketing & Design Copywriting Guide

This guide contains the product positioning, sales copy, and AI image generator prompts for the two main access packages: **Match Pass** and **Tournament Pass**.

---

## 1. Product Positioning & Copywriting

### Package A: Match Pass (Single Match Access)
*Designed for casual viewers or fans tuning in for a specific match.*

* **Marketing Title:** `Match Pass: The 90-Min Riot` (or `Match Pass: Single Match Access`)
* **Tagline:** *"Step past the corporate paywall. One ticket, zero contracts, pure action."*
* **Core Pitch:**
  > "Stop letting corporate media networks dictate how you watch your team. The StreamZone Match Pass gives you instant, raw access to a single live event broadcast in full HD with zero fluff. No subscription traps, no recurring fees. Just the game, by the fans, for the fans."
* **Features Checklist:**
  * [x] **1 Screen Access:** Perfect for personal viewing on phone, tablet, or laptop.
  * [x] **1080p HD, 60 FPS:** Full fidelity broadcast.
  * [x] **Ad-Shield Compatible:** Custom player routing designed to work flawlessly with privacy adblockers.
  * [x] **Instant Digital Delivery:** Access code delivered via WhatsApp in under 3 minutes.
* **Pricing Copy:** *"Less than the price of a stadium coffee. Buy once, watch the game."*

---

### Package B: Tournament Pass (Season/Cup Access)
*Designed for hardcore supporters who want to watch the entire campaign.*

* **Marketing Title:** `Tournament Pass: Total Access` (or `Tournament Pass: Full Cup Pass`)
* **Tagline:** *"Own the pitch. Every game, every tackle, all the way to the final whistle."*
* **Core Pitch:**
  > "For the true supporters who won't miss a single header. The Tournament Pass unlocks every live match of the tournament, full replay archives, and priority server routing. Save over 40% compared to buying single passes and secure your spot in the virtual stadium."
* **Features Checklist:**
  * [x] **2 Simultaneous Screens:** Watch on your TV and phone at the same time, or share with a friend.
  * [x] **Priority Routing:** Reserved high-bandwidth stream channels to avoid buffer lag during peak traffic.
  * [x] **Live Match Alerts:** Instant push/SMS notifications when your team kicks off.
  * [x] **Full Replay Archive:** Rewind and rewatch key games any time.
* **Pricing Copy:** *"One pass, complete freedom. The ultimate value pack for true fans."*
